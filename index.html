Yoshi

Yoshi is an adorable green dinosaur created by Shigefumi Hino from the company Nintendo. Yoshi is capable of shooting small dinosaur eggs and flutter kicking while jumping high in the air. Yoshi is also known as an ally of Mario.

Yoshi made its debut in the 1990 version of Super Mario World and has also made appearances in other games such as Mario Party, Mario Kart, and Super Smash Bros. Who doesn't love Yoshi?
